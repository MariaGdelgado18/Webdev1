The Moon Phases

The Moon goes through diffrent phases!

      8 main phases of the moon: new moon, waxing crescent, first quarter, waxing gibbous, full moon, waning gibbous, third quarter, and waning crescent
      These phases repeat themselves about once every 29.5 days.
      There are also four secondary phases: Waxing Crescent, Waxing Gibbous, Waning Gibbous, and Waning Crescent.

1969 First Landing of the Moon by Neil Armstrong

On July 20th 1969, The spacecraft Apollo 11 decided to go to the moon, To complete a mission. Astronauts Neil Armstrong,Buzz Aldrin and Micheal Collins were sent to the moon to make history. It took them from 9:32am to 10:56 for Neil to plant the first human foot into a new atmosphere.
