Luna's Library

"If you want the best books in town, Head to Luna's Library, She sure is a pal!"

Luna's Library gives a wide variety of collection of books ranged from all ages. We provide here a since place if you would want to relax or do homework as well we offer tutor in case you would like some help with school.

This Library was opened in 1991 by It's owner Luna, She chose to open this libary as a chance for people to adapt more with reading as well have a quiet place to do so. There is in total of 10 workers there to help Luna run her precious library. Luna always had the dream to open her own Library so she chose to study education in college and saved money working several jobs and her dreams came true with Luna's Library.
